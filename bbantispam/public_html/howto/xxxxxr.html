Let me introduce X****r

X****r bot is your main enemy. Among other things, he can:

  • find the forum on your site,
  • select the best forum category to post to,
  • decode many types of CAPTCHA,
  • pass through javascript protection,
  • complete email verification,
  • create accounts on free mail services.

Fortunately, X****r is quite expensive and his site isn't translated to English yet.

Recommendation: if you write your own protection, expect that spam bots are smart and humans help them.
