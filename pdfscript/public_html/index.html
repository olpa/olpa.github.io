PDFscript: create big books and small articles

I believe that the world needs a human-friendly PDF generation library. Instead of low-level management—

“put a line of text in that location using this bolded text”

—I prefers to say just:

“I need an article. Here is the title, here are the paragraphs.”

In a pseudocode:

doc = pdfscript.document('article')
doc.title('De finibus bonorum et malorum')
doc.para('Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, ...')
doc.para('Duis autem vel eum iriure dolor ...')
doc.para('Ut wisi enim ad minim veniam, ...')
...
