Spam words

Some loosers believe in financial pyramids and want to spam your forum. Fortunately, they don't have money to make it automatically. Unfortunately, they sometimes register and spam.

It doesn't make much harm, but anyway unpleasant. If you are tired of “paypal” postings, stop them.

Go to the page “Word Censors” and say to replace “paypal” with “http://paypal.bbspam.com/”.


 “Paypal” and “WMZ” are spam words

What happens? You use an undocumented feature of Links Rejector. If a link contains “bbspam.com”, then Links Rejector rejects the posting, even if it comes from a registered user.
